There was a family with the surname Mammone, living in the same vicinity, who worked for a political party in the election campaigns etc. who were convincing the lady to monitor our family. Later I came to know that she got a call from a group of people to visit their office and start working for them. She was scared to go there by herself and I had to take someone with her. Frequently, group of men with bizarre appearances (could be

reasonably described as hooligans) would drive around our house. One of those instances when I stopped them and asked if I could help them because the way they were driving was a "No through road". They intentionally tried to pick a fight. With a heavy heart, we left the area and moved to 2 old Beecroft Road Cheltenham NSW, where my daughter joined the local girl's high school. We were not spared there too. My post box got broken; my letters were opened and thrown in front of my house. Finally I reported the incident to the local police. Constable Michelle Parragne wrote my incident report then (I only reported the breaking in of my post box).While in the area, I soon became aware of two houses (addresses no. l and no.4) belonging to immigrants with Teenage children. These young people were coerced into working in surveillance (as a 'watchdog') for the government .Something I have not seen happening in any other country.
